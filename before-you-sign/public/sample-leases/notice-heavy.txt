Initial term: 12 months from the lease start date.
Renewal: Unless either party provides written notice at least 90 days before the end of the initial term, the lease automatically renews on a month-to-month basis on the same terms except rent may be adjusted.
Rent increases: The landlord may increase rent with not less than 45 days' written notice prior to the effective date of the increase.
Tenant notice to vacate: Tenant must provide at least 60 days' written notice before the intended move-out date.
Landlord notice to terminate month-to-month tenancy: Landlord will provide notice in accordance with applicable law, and in no case less than 30 days where permitted.
Entry: Landlord may enter with 24 hours' notice for maintenance, inspections, or showing the unit to prospective tenants after notice of non-renewal.
